Weathering You!

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Engine: React/API
  • Project date: June 2020

A React project powered by Weather API, simple and neat looking. At the start, it will ask for your location, if allowed, it can then show the weather of your current city/location. The app also has other cities (Tokyo, Berlin, Rome, etc.) for your viewing.
Bonus: The weather data is actually 5 days forecast, with the first "cloud" being today!
